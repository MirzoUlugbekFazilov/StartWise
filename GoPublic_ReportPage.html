üéØ The Problem

University students, particularly in STEM fields, struggle to create effective study plans that align with their individual learning pace, course schedules, and exam dates. Current solutions are generic and don't adapt to student performance or changing priorities.

This results in inefficient studying, missed topics, last-minute cramming, and ultimately lower academic performance. Students need a personalized, intelligent system that acts as their study coach.

üí° Our Solution

AI-Powered Study Planner uses machine learning to create dynamic, personalized learning paths for each student. The platform:

  • Analyzes student goals, current performance, and learning pace
  • Generates optimized study schedules that adapt in real-time
  • Integrates with calendars and campus LMS systems
  • Uses spaced repetition algorithms for better retention
  • Provides visual progress dashboards and smart reminders
  • Automatically adjusts based on quiz results and self-assessments

üìä Market Opportunity

The global EdTech market is projected to reach $404 billion by 2025, with personalized learning solutions growing at 15% CAGR. Our initial target market includes:

  • Primary: 20M+ university students in the US (STEM focus)
  • Secondary: Professional learners and certification seekers
  • Expansion: Global markets with English-speaking student populations

Competitors like Quizlet and Khan Academy lack personalized AI-driven scheduling. We differentiate through calendar integration, adaptive learning paths, and real-time performance tracking.

üöÄ Traction & Validation

We've completed a successful pilot program with promising results:

  • 50 beta users tested the MVP over 8 weeks
  • 30% improvement in study consistency and time management
  • 4.7/5 average user satisfaction rating
  • 85% of users indicated they would pay for the service
  • 2 partnerships in discussion with university study centers

We're now ready to scale with a full product launch targeting 1,000 paying users in the first 6 months.

üíº Business Model

Freemium SaaS model with multiple revenue streams:

  • Free Tier: Basic study planning and progress tracking
  • Premium ($9.99/mo): AI scheduling, calendar sync, unlimited quizzes
  • Pro ($19.99/mo): Advanced analytics, custom study paths, priority support
  • B2B/University: Site licenses and institutional partnerships

Projected revenue: $50K in Year 1, $500K in Year 2, $2M in Year 3 based on conservative conversion rates of 10% free-to-paid.
